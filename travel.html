All About Traveling Around Greece!

Main Information

Greece is a country located in southeastern europe. Greece is made up of many islands which are located between the Aegean and Ionian seas. There are about 6,000 islands which make up greece. On the other hand, only 227 are populated the rest are inhabited.

Top 5 Greek Islands to Visit

  1. Santorini
  2. Mykonos
  3. Crete
  4. Naxos
  5. Antiparos

Traditional Foods

Greece's national dish is called the "Moussaka" . The Moussaka consists of various layers of fried aubergine, minced meat, potatoes. This famous dish is served with a creamy bechamel sauce. It is also usually baked until it is golden brown. Some other traditional greek dishes are...

  • The taramasalata
  • Olives and olive oil
  • Dolmades
  • Grilled Meat
  • Fresh Fish

Activities & Tours

In Greece, there are many unique things to do in the various beautiful islands. For example, the most popular things or tours to take in Athens are, touring acropolis, taking a bike tour around the historical sites of athens and plaka, taking a traditional greek cooking lesson, or even going to the meteora monasteries!


When in Santorini you could take a tour of the volcanoes and hot springs or take a sunset boat tour and enjoy the beautiful painted skies beside the beautiful aqua colored ocean. By taking boat tours, you get to admire the beauty of the bright blue ocean and the nature there is around the islands. It is also relaxing and a great way to spend your day!


If you take a visit to Mykonos, you should defenitely take a jeep tour of the island, visit the different beaches and walk around, learn about the culture. The jeep tour is a great opportunity too really get to know the island, while being comfortable. It also allows you to explore all different parts of the island, meaning you can go from the village to the different beaches.
